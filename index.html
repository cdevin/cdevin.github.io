Coline Devin

I am a staff research scientist at Google DeepMind Roboticss. I completed my PhD in 2020 at UC Berkeley, advised by Professors Sergey Levine, Pieter Abbeel and Trevor Darrell, and supported by the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship. I have previously interned at OSARO, Google Brain, and DeepMind. I completed my B.S. in Computer Science at Harvey Mudd College.
